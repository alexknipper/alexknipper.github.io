Welcome!

My name is Alex Knipper, and I'm a MS & PhD student in the department of Computer Science & Software Engineering at Auburn University, working under Dr. Shubhra Kanti Karmaker (Santu).

My general research interests cover the conceptualization and development of systems that can accurately exhibit (and exceed) human-level intelligence. This includes a light interest in behavioral science and neuroscience-related fields, where I believe some of the best inspiration can be drawn from.

More specifically, my current research interests mainly sit between Big Data, Natural Language Processing, and Neuromorphic Computing. More details on my current research can be found in the Projects and Publications sections.
